System Architecture

User Roles & Capabilities

Defining the core interactions and operational boundaries for every system participant in TRACE EM.

Primary User
👤

Registered Customer

  • Order Life-Cycle Management

    Seamlessly place, monitor, and confirm receipt of orders through an intuitive end-to-end interface.

  • Real-time Visualization

    Track drivers on a high-fidelity live map with millisecond-precision updates and ETA calculations.

  • Engagement & Feedback

    Rate delivery experiences and report issues directly through the integrated, AI-assisted complaint system.

  • Profile Control

    Comprehensive management of delivery addresses, saved locations, and granular notification settings.

Control Plane
🛡️

System Administrator

  • Fleet & User Moderation

    Verify driver credentials, manage user access levels, and maintain community standards across the platform.

  • Operational Dashboard

    Real-time analytics on order volume, driver efficiency, and system health through interactive heatmaps.

  • Conflict Resolution

    Review and resolve escalated complaints, manage refund requests, and oversee the rating integrity system.

  • Financial Oversight

    Manage receipts, track revenue flows, and audit transaction logs for transparent fiscal management.
